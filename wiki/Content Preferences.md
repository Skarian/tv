## Movies And TV Series

Movies and scripted TV series should use [[Stremio]] by default. Do not ask which app/service to use for movie or TV-series requests.

For Dragon Ball Z requests, prefer the original `Dragon Ball Z` series over `Dragon Ball Z Kai` unless the user explicitly asks for Kai.

Known Stremio mapping:

- "the episode where Goku becomes a Super Saiyan" means original `Dragon Ball Z` episode 95, `Transformed at Last`; the prior Cinemeta mapping used `tt0214341:3:21`.
